Hemoglobin

Hemoglobin is described in detail in rcbc.org molecule of the month article.

Hemoglobin is the protein that makes blood red. It is composed of four protein chains, two alpha chains and two beta chains, each with a ring-like heme group containing an iron atom. The four chains are colored red green blue and yellow. Oxygen binds reversibly to these iron atoms and is transported through blood.
